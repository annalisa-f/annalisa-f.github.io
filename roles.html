What can you do?

First Assistant Director

Help with organisation, creative leading, and yell at people on set!

Assistant Camera x2

Collaborate on the technical side of our film, practice using some of the flash gear, and keep busy on set!

Sound Recordist

Capture some great audio, hold the boom, and probably be the most chill person there.

Grip

Play with cool camera rigs and help to figure out how we can get that tricky shot.

Gaffer & Best Boy

Light us up! Work with the DOP to make all the shots look pretty.

Production Designer

Lead the art department with the director, helping with costumes, set, makeup and more!

Art Department Assist

Help the Production Designer and Director make sure that everything is looking great on the day.

Lead VFX

Help us bring the stormy end of our film to life! Collaborate on planning the shots & edit to make it look great.

Assistant Editor

Help the editor put together cuts, colour grade, and bring the whole thing to life!

Sound Mixer

Help us bring it to life in the edit with a great audio mix.
